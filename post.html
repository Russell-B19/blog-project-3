Strongest Village in Naruto

Could any village come close to beating the Hidden Leaf Village? This is the question that many people have once they start watching Naruto. The Hidden Leaf, Hidden Cloud and Hidden Sand Village are all villages to be feared as they have some very capable ninja within them. The only issue is that the Leaf Village is much too powerful to scale against the other villages.
